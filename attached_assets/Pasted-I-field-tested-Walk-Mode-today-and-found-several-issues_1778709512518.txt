I field-tested Walk Mode today and found several issues that need debugging and refinement. Please review the Walk Mode logic holistically, especially map pins, GPS/story matching, story loading, and playback controls.

Key issues to address:

1. Pin styling changed unexpectedly
- Pins are now showing as tiny orange dots.
- Please restore them to the larger, more visible pin style we had previously, or create a clearer walk-mode pin style that is easy to see while walking outdoors.

2. Narrated story locations are not precise enough
- My blue-dot location is accurate, but the narrated stories are often for places that are one street/block away, or down side streets I am not actually walking on.
- Please tighten the matching logic so narration prioritizes stories on or very near the user’s actual current street/path.
- Avoid triggering stories simply because they are nearby “as the crow flies” if they are not on the user’s current route or block.

3. Walk Mode should prioritize the direction of travel
- The app is loading or narrating stories from blocks away, including in the opposite direction from where I am walking.
- Please refine the loading logic so Walk Mode focuses on stories ahead of the user, based on heading/direction of travel.
- Stories behind the user or far off to the side should be deprioritized unless there are no better nearby options.

4. Map and story loading do not keep up during the walk
- As I walk, the map does not consistently follow or “catch up” to my current location.
- New stories do not reliably load as I move.
- I often have to stop Walk Mode and restart it to get fresh nearby stories.
- Please make Walk Mode continuously refresh based on updated GPS location, without requiring a restart.

5. Add manual pin playback controls
- I want to be able to tap/select any visible pin and manually play its narrative.
- If a story has already played, I should be able to restart/replay it from the pin.
- Please add clear UI behavior for selected pins, including a play/replay option.

Overall goal:
Walk Mode should feel reliable while walking in a city grid. It should follow the user smoothly, prioritize stories on the user’s current path and direction of travel, avoid narrating irrelevant nearby side-street stories, and allow manual replay from pins.

Please inspect the current Walk Mode implementation, identify the likely causes, and propose/code fixes carefully without breaking existing map or story functionality.
